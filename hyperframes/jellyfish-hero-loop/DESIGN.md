## Style Prompt

Create a warm, light, personal-blog hero loop for Jellyfish's Hugo site. The visual language should feel like a gentle personal archive: soft daylight, translucent glass, pale blue-green atmosphere, tiny rose accents, slow floating cards, and a quiet sense of recording, sharing, and exploring. The animation is ambient background, not a promotional spot.

## Colors

- `#f8f5ee` page warmth and soft paper light
- `#eaf7f4` mint glass haze
- `#dce8ff` pale sky reflections
- `#2f58a5` restrained Jellyfish blue for depth lines
- `#f2a9ce` tiny rose particle accents

## Typography

- Primary: `Inter`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Text is minimal and decorative only; this video should not compete with the real hero copy.

## Motion Rules

- Slow, breathable motion over exactly 10 seconds.
- Loop seamlessly: end state visually returns to the opening state.
- Use transforms and opacity only where possible.
- No hard flashes, no fast cuts, no exploding transitions.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not make it dark, cyberpunk, neon-heavy, or commercial.
- Do not place readable promotional captions over the center.
- Do not obscure the central hero title area.
- Do not use intense glow, large bokeh blobs, or noisy particle storms.
- Do not embed autoplay music.
