## Style Prompt

Create an ambient loop for the Jellyfish blog Resources and Tools pages. The scene is a gentle personal bookmark universe: translucent cards, abstract tags, pale connection lines, and a quiet search scan that suggests knowledge, tools, and inspiration being organized. It should feel like a private collection, not a SaaS dashboard.

## Colors

- `#f8f5ee` warm paper background
- `#ebf8f5` mint glass atmosphere
- `#dce9ff` soft blue reflection
- `#2f58a5` restrained blue for lines and tag depth
- `#6bcfc5` mint connection highlights
- `#f0a6c9` tiny rose accents

## Typography

- Primary: `Inter`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Only short abstract labels are allowed: `links`, `tools`, `notes`, `ideas`, `index`.

## Motion Rules

- 8 seconds, seamless loop.
- Cards slowly come into focus, then float subtly.
- A search scan crosses once from left to right and fades into the loop.
- Connection lines pulse gently, never flash.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not show real website logos.
- Do not make it dark, cyberpunk, neon, or corporate.
- Do not use large promotional text.
- Do not create busy particles or rapid transitions.
- Do not embed autoplay music.
