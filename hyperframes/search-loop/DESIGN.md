## Style Prompt

Create an ambient loop for the Jellyfish blog search page. The scene shows gentle discovery: a soft search input, tiny keyword fragments, abstract article/resource/tool/game cards, and light points that connect clues into a calm search result list. It should feel warm, personal, and useful, not like a commercial product demo.

## Colors

- `#f8f5ee` warm paper background
- `#edf9f6` mint glass atmosphere
- `#e2ecff` soft blue reflection
- `#315aa4` restrained blue for search lines and card depth
- `#6bcfc5` mint clue highlights
- `#f0a6c9` tiny rose accents

## Typography

- Primary: `Inter`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Only short labels are allowed: `文章`, `资源`, `工具`, `游戏`.
- No long readable text or marketing copy.

## Motion Rules

- 7 seconds, seamless loop.
- Search input gently breathes while cards float slowly.
- Keyword dots travel from the input area toward cards, then settle into a soft result list.
- Connection lines and scan highlights must stay subtle.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not show real website names, logos, or readable article titles.
- Do not make the scene dark, cyberpunk, neon, or corporate.
- Do not use large captions or promotional text.
- Do not create noisy particles or fast transitions.
- Do not embed autoplay music.
