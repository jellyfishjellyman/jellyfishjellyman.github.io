## Style Prompt

Create a very quiet ambient loop for Jellyfish blog post pages. The scene should feel like warm paper, soft reading light, ink traces, and thoughts being gently organized. It is meant for article title areas or card hover backgrounds, so it must remain pale and low contrast enough to keep titles and summaries readable.

## Colors

- `#f8f5ee` warm paper base
- `#fffdf8` soft page surface
- `#e5f5f1` pale mint reading glow
- `#dfeaff` soft blue reflection
- `#315aa4` restrained ink-blue line accents
- `#f0a6c9` tiny rose particles

## Typography

- Primary: `Inter`, `Arial`, sans-serif`
- No readable article text.
- Only abstract line marks are allowed.

## Motion Rules

- 6 seconds, seamless loop.
- Thin writing lines gently appear, drift, and soften.
- One broad reading-light sweep crosses the page slowly.
- A few mint and rose particles float with very small motion.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not use large captions, readable paragraphs, or marketing copy.
- Do not make high contrast ink blocks that compete with post titles.
- Do not use dramatic camera moves, bright flares, or hard cuts.
- Do not make the scene dark, cyberpunk, or commercial.
- Do not embed autoplay music.
